Ingredients:
 - 1 roll of sausage
 - 1 onion, diced
 - 3 stalks celery, chopped
 - 1/2 green pepper, chopped
 - 1 envelope chicken and noodle soup, dry
 - 1 C raw rice
 - 2 C water

Directions:
 1. Cook the sausage and vegetables together until sausage is done.
 2. Combine sausage, vegetables, soup mix, rice, and water in 9 X 13 baking casserole dish.
 3. Cover with foil. Bake 50 min. 
 4. Uncover and bake additional 20 min.
